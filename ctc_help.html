What is the CTC Format?

CTC stands for Condition-Transition-Consequence. It is a format used to describe risks in a structured way.
The Condition describes the situation or context that could lead to a risk event.
The Transition outlines the specific event or change that could occur.
The Consequence details the potential impact or outcome if the risk event occurs.
